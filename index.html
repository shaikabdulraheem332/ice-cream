🍨 Product Description:

Ice cream has a rich, creamy texture that melts in your mouth.
Its taste can range from simple and milky to intense and flavorful depending on the ingredients used.
The smoothness comes from the blending of fat, air, and ice crystals during the freezing process.

🍧 Types of Ice Cream

  • Soft serve – Light, airy, and served fresh from machines
  • Gelato – Italian-style, denser and more flavorful
  • Sorbet – Dairy-free, made with fruit and sugar.
  • Kulfi – Traditional Indian ice cream, dense and often flavored with cardamom or nuts .

🍦 Popular Flavors

  1. Vanilla – Classic, mild, and creamy.
  2. Chocolate – Rich and slightly bitter-sweet
  3. Strawberry – Fruity and refreshing
  4. Mango – Sweet and tropical.
  5. Butterscotch – Sweet with a caramel-like taste
